## About This Repository

This repository contains my NTCC term paper on **AI-driven predictive maintenance in the aviation sector**, submitted as part of the B.Tech CSE curriculum at Amity University, Noida.

The paper explores how Artificial Intelligence — specifically Machine Learning and Deep Learning — can transform aircraft maintenance from a reactive, schedule-based process into an intelligent, condition-based system that predicts failures before they happen.

## What the Paper Covers

- **Background** — Evolution of aviation maintenance from reactive to predictive
- **Predictive Maintenance** — How it works and why it matters
- **AI & ML Techniques** — Anomaly detection, RUL estimation, LSTM networks
- **Data in Aviation** — Types of data, preprocessing, feature engineering
- **Real-World Case Studies** — Delta Air Lines APEX, Lufthansa AVIATAR, United Airlines
- **Challenges & Ethics** — Data privacy, algorithmic transparency, regulation
- **Future Scope** — Digital Twins, Explainable AI, Autonomous Maintenance Systems

## Key Highlights from Research

| Airline | AI System | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Delta Air Lines | APEX | Flight cancellations dropped from 5,600 → 55 per year |
| Lufthansa Technik | AVIATAR | 30% reduction in unscheduled maintenance |
| United Airlines | Engine Health AI | Multi-million dollar annual fuel savings |
